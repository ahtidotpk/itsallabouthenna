About Us

We are Ezzah and Roha, and for over last four years we have been adorning brides and inspiring artists with our mehndi designs. We have a very special relationship with doing henna/mehndi. We are self-taught henna artist practicing this art for the last 4-5 years and have always been committed to providing artistic, professional services and making our clients experience with henna/mehndi a unique, enjoyable and an unforgettable one. This exquisite form of body art is part of our cultural heritage and we are proud to be able to decorate each bride-to-be with a pattern unique to her personality, including motifs that express her inner dreams and desires. We are providing this service in Karachi. Home charges will be applied according to the area. We use good quality and chemical free henna that leaves a very dark and long lasting stain on hands. We can do Mehndi/Henna for just about any occasion. We Specialize in Bridal Mehndi, Sangeet Parties, Engagements, Baby Showers, Birthday Parties, Ladies Night outs, School Events, Corporate Events and Fundraisers. We love to extend this art form onto cakes and other mediums like wood, glass and candles.
